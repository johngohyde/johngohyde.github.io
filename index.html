Who I Am

I’m an execution-focused leader with a track record of bringing bold ideas to life — and scaling them with precision. Over the last decade, I’ve led high-impact work across industries including tech, hospitality, manufacturing, real estate development, and global supply chains. From building companies from scratch to transforming underperforming assets, I specialize in turning vision into operational reality. But here’s what I believe sets meaningful work apart — relationships. In a future shaped by automation and AI, human connection remains the one thing that can’t be outsourced. It’s also the most powerful driver of trust, momentum, and lasting impact. I build companies and lead teams through the lens of that belief — knowing that strong relationships are not a soft skill, but a strategic one. Whether scaling a Series B startup, renovating historic hotels, or managing complex development projects, my approach is grounded in clarity, systems thinking, and deep relational trust. I know how to move between strategy and action — building the teams, processes, and partnerships needed to create real, lasting value. I serve on the boards of multiple nonprofits, guiding mission-aligned organizations toward sustainable growth. Earlier in my career, I toured with and managed a nationally performing band — a creative experience that sharpened my instincts around brand, cohesion, and leadership under pressure. This site highlights the ventures I've helped shape and the results we've driven — because good ideas are just the beginning. The work is in the build. And at the center of it all? People.
